# 📍 Spots Help Centre

The Spots module is your private, van-aware location database and route planning engine — all in one place. Browse thousands of park-ups on a live map, plan multi-stop routes with real-time weather and fuel estimates, and build your own personal collection of favourite places.

Everything in Spots is stored locally in a high-performance on-device database. Your coordinates never leave your device.

## How Spots Connects to the Rest of the App

| Spots feature | Connects to |
|---|---|
| **My Places** | Syncs across iCloud; spots survive phone restores |
| **Spot detail ratings** | Mirror your Check-in ratings when promoted to a Trip Check-in |
| **Trip Planner waypoints** | Can be promoted to full Trip Check-ins inside an Expedition |
| **Van profile dimensions** | Used to auto-filter spots that don't fit your vehicle |
| **Route fuel estimate** | Pulled from your Money ledger's average fuel cost per km/mile |
| **Route Conditions weather** | Same engine as the Trips weather card on the Dashboard |
