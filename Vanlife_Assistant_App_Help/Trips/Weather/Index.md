# 🌤️ Weather: Help Index

Weather is woven through the entire app — the Trips Dashboard tracks it in real time, check-ins capture it historically, the Route Conditions sheet previews it along your planned journey, and the Van Brain can trigger HomeKit automations when conditions change. This section covers all of it.
