# Emergency Mode: What it is and how to use it 🚨

**Emergency Mode** transforms your phone into a crisis survival tool. By removing all distractions, menus, and standard app features, it presents a highly visible, locked-down screen with only the tools you need to survive, signal for help, and communicate with rescue services.

## 🛡️ How to use the Dashboard

### 1. Smart Dialling
When you cross borders, emergency numbers change (e.g., 999 in the UK, 112 in France, 911 in the USA). 
* You do **not** need to remember local numbers. 
* The giant red **Call Emergency** button automatically detects your current country via GPS and sets up the correct local emergency number. Just tap it to dial.

### 2. Precise Location Beacon
Rescue services will ask where you are. The dashboard prominently displays:
* Your exact **Latitude and Longitude** coordinates.
* Your current **Altitude** (crucial for mountain rescues).
* Your **Nearest Address / Place Name**.
* **what3words Integration:** Tap the `/// what3words` button to instantly open the what3words app (or website) at your exact location to generate a 3-word location identifier for emergency services.
* *Need to text your location?* Tap the **Copy Coords** button to instantly copy your GPS data to your clipboard.

### 3. Visual & Audio SOS Signals
If you are stranded off-grid, you can use your phone as a physical beacon to guide rescuers:
* **Siren:** Tap the **Siren** button to play a piercing, continuous alarm sound from your device's speakers. 
* **Torch & SOS Flash:** Tap the Torch icon to turn on your flashlight. **Long-press** the Torch button to activate **SOS Signal**—this will flash your phone's camera light AND strobe your phone screen bright white in a continuous Morse code S-O-S pattern (`... --- ...`).

### 4. Progressive Document Disclosure (The Vault)
To keep the screen clean during a panic, your documents are hidden by default. However, Vanlife Assistant is smart:
* If you tap the **Medical** or **Fire** call buttons, a blue **Documents** button will dynamically appear. Tapping it will instantly bring up your Medical documents (like insurance and GHIC cards).
* If you tap **Police**, it prepares your ID and Passports.
* If you tap **Recovery**, it prepares your Vehicle Registration and V5C. 
*(This uses the secure [[What is Border Mode and how do I use it?|Border Mode]] presentation style).*

## 🔒 Safe Exiting
Because you don't want to accidentally close this screen while fumbling in an emergency, standard iOS swipe-to-dismiss gestures are disabled. 

To exit Emergency Mode, you must explicitly tap the **EXIT** button in the top right corner and confirm your choice. Exiting will also safely turn off any active Sirens or SOS flashlight strobes.
