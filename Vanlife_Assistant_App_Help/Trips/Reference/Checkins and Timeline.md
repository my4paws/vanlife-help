# 🏕️ Check-ins and Timeline

## Overview

Your **Timeline** is the chronological story of your vanlife. While the app tracks your driving and spending automatically, a **Check-in** is your way of manually saving a specific location—like a breathtaking wild camping spot, a free Aire with a stunning view, or a paid campsite with a hot shower you've earned.

## How It Works

Whenever you park up somewhere you want to remember, open the Trips Dashboard and tap the **+ Check In** button.

The Check-in Wizard will let you log:

- **Location Name:** (e.g., "Cliffside Parkup overlooking the ocean").
- **Place Type:** Choose from the five types below—each one gets its own colour and icon on your map so you can tell your stops apart at a glance.
- **Rating:** 1 to 5 stars.
- **Photos:** A hero image from your camera roll to capture the moment.
- **Journal Notes:** (e.g., "Great 4G signal here, but very windy at night!").

Once saved, this Check-in is dropped onto your [[Unified History Map]] and added to the chronological Timeline of your current [[Creating a Trip|Expedition]].

### The Five Place Types

Every Check-in belongs to one of these types. They're deliberately focused on the spots vanlifers actually stay—and each one appears as a distinct coloured pin on your map so your history is readable at a glance.

|Type|Map Colour|Best used for|
|---|---|---|
|🗺️ **Unspecified**|Grey|Anything that doesn't fit the others—a viewpoint, a mechanic, a water fill-up|
|🌲 **Wild Camp**|Green|Off-grid, free overnight spots in nature with no official designation|
|🚐 **Free Aire**|Teal|Official designated van/motorhome parking areas at no cost|
|🚐 **Paid Aire**|Red|Official aire-style areas with a nightly fee|
|⛺ **Campsite**|Indigo|Formal, managed campsites with reception and facilities|

> **Pro tip:** Use the **Services** grid when logging to record exactly what each spot offered—electricity, water, showers, waste disposal, and more. This makes every stop searchable later, so you can find your best-equipped stops in seconds. See [[Check-in Services and Journaling]] for the full breakdown.

### The "Stow to Whiteboard" Trick

Did you find a great spot, but notice the van needs a repair while you're there? You can use voice or the UI to link a [[Using the Whiteboard|Whiteboard Task]] directly to a Check-in, so you always remember _where_ an issue happened.

## Common Questions

**Are my Check-ins private?** Yes. Unlike public apps like Park4Night, your Check-ins in Vanlife Assistant are 100% private and stored in your personal iCloud database. You can save your secret, untouched wild camping spots without any worry of them getting crowded!

**Can I log a Check-in without cell service?** Absolutely. The offline engine will grab your raw GPS coordinates immediately. Once you drive back into cell service, the app's geocoder will silently update the Check-in with the correct street address, town, and country name in the background.

## Troubleshooting

### "The location of my Check-in is slightly off."

If you are deep in a forest, GPS accuracy can drop to a 50-metre radius.

1. Open the Check-in from your Timeline.
2. Tap **Edit**.
3. Tap the mini-map to open the **Flexible Location Picker**.
4. Simply drag the pin to the exact spot you parked and hit save.
