# 🎛️ The Trips Dashboard

## Overview
The **Trips Dashboard** is the command centre for your rolling home. Vanlife isn't just about driving from A to B; it's about the places you stay, the borders you cross, and the milestones you hit along the way. 

Rather than burying your travel data in menus, the Dashboard displays your live metrics on a series of interactive, beautifully designed "Cards" that adapt to what you are currently doing.

## How It Works: The Dashboard Cards

### 1. The Journey Card (The Hero Card)
Sitting right at the top of your screen, the Journey Card gives you the immediate status of your expedition. It is distinctly designed with **two sides**—simply tap anywhere on the card to flip it over!

**The Front (Drive & Prep):**
* **Days on the Road:** A massive, animated counter showing exactly how long you've been living the dream.
* **The Drive Recorder Dot:** Right in the top centre, you'll see a small indicator dot. When this dot is pulsing green, the [[Drive Recorder]] is actively logging your route. Tap the dot to see exactly what tracking mode is running.
* **Parking Status (Top Left):** * If you are moving, this shows a subtle "Parking" button. Tap it the moment you pull into a spot to instantly log your exact GPS coordinates. 
    * If you are already parked, this turns blue, shows you exactly how long you've been parked there, and—most importantly—provides a menu with **Walking Directions** to guide you back to your van via Apple Maps if you get lost on a hike!
* **Pre-Flight / Ready (Top Right):** A quick safety check. If you haven't done a vehicle check recently, this warns you to do a "Pre-Flight". Tapping it opens the [[Pre-Flight Checklist]]. Once completed, it glows green and says "Ready" to roll.

**The Back (Smart Modes & Milestones):**
* **If SmartVan is Enabled:** The back of the card transforms into your van's control panel. You can trigger your Smart Modes (Driving, Camped, Sleep, Stealth) or access your favorite accessories. 
  👉 *Safety Interlock:* If you try to tap "Driving" mode but your van's sensors report an open window or unlocked door, the app will block the mode change and instantly alert you!
* **If SmartVan is Disabled:** The back displays your upcoming travel milestones (e.g., "Day 100 is in 12 days") and yearly vanlife anniversaries.

### 2. The Unified History Map & Search
Directly below your current trips is your interactive map. 
* **Full Screen Mode:** Tap the crosshair icon in the top right to expand the map and explore your entire route.
* **Deep Search:** The search bar below the map is incredibly powerful. You can search your [[Checkins and Timeline|Check-ins]] by location name or your personal journal notes. *Pro Tip:* You can even search by facility! Type "paid electric", "free water", or "laundry" to instantly filter your map to spots that have exactly what you need.

### 3. Schengen Status Card
If you have non-EU travellers in your crew, this card is a lifesaver. It features a beautiful animated "liquid wave" donut chart showing exactly how many of your 90 visa-free days you have left. 
👉 *For a deep dive into the complex math running this card, see the [[Schengen Calculator]] guide.*

### 4. Visited Countries Card
A beautiful grid of flags representing every country you have entered. The app automatically knows you've visited a country if you log a Check-in, record a financial expense, or if the background GPS detects a border crossing.
* **Tap a flag** to see the country name.
* **Long-press a flag** to instantly filter your Unified History Map to only show routes and stops within that specific country!
