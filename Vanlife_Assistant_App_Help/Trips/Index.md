# 🗺️ Trips & Navigation

Welcome to the **Trips** module — the digital memory bank for your vanlife adventures.

Whether you are logging a quick weekend getaway or a multi-year pan-American expedition, this module tracks where you've been, how high you climbed, and the incredible spots you slept along the way.

Because vanlife takes you off the grid, the entire Trips engine is built to work offline. It will securely record your GPS coordinates and cache your check-ins, syncing everything to your iCloud map the moment you find cell service.
