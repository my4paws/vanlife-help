# Adding Transactions 💸

The **Ledger** is the financial heart of Vanlife Assistant. Every coffee you buy, every tank of diesel you fill, and every freelance invoice you get paid feeds into the app's powerful Runway and Scenario engines. 

Because vanlifers have unique financial needs—like crossing borders, splitting grocery bills, and tracking vehicle costs—we built the transaction editor to handle it all effortlessly.

To add a new transaction, open the **Money** tab, navigate to the **Ledger**, and tap the floating **+** button.

---

## 🔘 The Basics: Income, Expense, or Transfer
At the top of the editor, choose what kind of transaction you are logging:
* **Expense (Red):** Money leaving your pocket (e.g., Buying groceries).
* **Income (Green):** Money entering your pocket (e.g., Getting paid).
* **Transfer (Blue):** Moving money between your own accounts (e.g., Moving savings to your checking account). *Note: Transfers do not affect your Net Worth or Daily Burn Rate!*

## 🧮 The Built-In Calculator
You never need to jump to your phone's calculator app to split a bill again. 
If you tap the big **Amount** number, a full calculator slides up! You can type `15 + 8.50`, hit the `=` button, and tap **Confirm Amount** to drop it straight into the ledger.

## 🌍 Multi-Currency & Auto-Detection
Travelling across borders? The app has your back. 

If you have Location Services enabled, **the app will automatically detect what country you are in and switch the transaction to the local currency!** * You enter the amount in the *local* currency (e.g., Euros).
* The app automatically fetches the latest exchange rate and shows you exactly how much that costs in your *Home* currency (e.g., GBP).
* You can tap the Exchange Rate to manually override it if your bank gave you a different rate.

## ✂️ Split Transactions
Have you ever gone to a supermarket and bought £50 of Groceries, £20 of Alcohol, and a £10 Engine Oil top-up on the same receipt? 

Instead of entering three separate transactions, toggle on **Split Transaction**.
1. Enter the total receipt amount at the top (£80).
2. Assign the first chunk (£50) to the *Groceries* category.
3. Tap **Add Split** and assign the next chunk (£20) to *Alcohol*.
4. The app will constantly do the math for you, showing you exactly how much is "Remaining to allocate" until the bar turns green!

## 🚐 Vehicle Expenses & Odometers
The Ledger is fully integrated with your Van Profile.
If you select a category that is flagged as a Vehicle Expense (like *Fuel*, *Maintenance*, or *Upgrades*), the app will ask you which **Vehicle** it belongs to and ask for your current **Odometer** reading. 
👉 *Logging your odometer here is what powers the beautiful Fuel Efficiency charts in the Van tab!*

## 📷 Smart Receipts (Glovebox Integration)
Paper receipts fade and get lost under the passenger seat.
Tap **Scan Receipt** to open the built-in document scanner. Snap a photo of your receipt, and the app will convert it to a crisp PDF. 
* **The Magic:** This receipt isn't just attached to the transaction; it is also automatically filed away into your secure [[How to securely store vehicle and travel documents|Glovebox]] under the "Receipts" tag for safekeeping!

## 📍 Location Auto-Tagging
If you are logging an expense on the go, the app will automatically tag the GPS coordinates and the name of the town you are in. You can also toggle **Is Waypoint (Check-in)** to have this expense show up as a pin on your [[Trips Index|Trip Map]]!
