# Managing Accounts 🏦

While the Ledger tracks your daily spending, the **Accounts** tab is your financial command center. This is where you track your total wealth across different banks, credit cards, cash stashes, and even the physical value of your van.

Keeping your account balances accurate is what allows the app to calculate your master [[Understanding Financial Scenarios|Runway]] and predict exactly how long you can stay on the road.

---

## ➕ Adding a New Account
To add an account, navigate to the **Accounts** tab (via the building icon on your main dashboard) and tap the **+** button. 

You can track almost anything: *Current Accounts*, *Savings*, *Credit Cards*, *Cash*, and even *Loans*. When you select the Account Type, the app will automatically organise it into **Assets** (money you have) or **Liabilities** (money you owe).

### The Golden Toggles
When creating an account, you will see two very important toggles:
1. **Include in Net Worth:** Does this account count towards your total wealth? *(You might turn this off for a shared joint account where the money isn't entirely yours).*
2. **Include in Forecast (Runway):** Can you actually spend this money to survive? *(For example, you might include a locked "Property" asset in your Net Worth, but turn OFF "Include in Forecast" because you can't buy diesel with a house!)*

### 📈 Interest Rates & Passive Income
If you are tracking a high-yield savings account or a loan, enter the **Interest Rate (% AER)**. The app will automatically calculate and display exactly how much money that account is earning (or costing) you daily, monthly, and yearly!

## 🔐 Secure Bank & Card Details (The Flip Card)
Vanlife often means booking ferries or paying for visas on your phone. Instead of digging through your physical wallet for your debit card, you can securely store your card details directly inside the account profile.

* **Front of Card:** Store your Account Name, Sort Code, and Account Number.
* **Back of Card:** Tap the card in the app to watch it physically flip over! Here, you can store your Long Card Number, Expiry, CVV, and PIN.

> **🛡️ Security Note:** Your sensitive banking data is never sent to our servers. In fact as a reminder, NOTHING is shared with us. We believe your data is yours and yours alone. It is securely encrypted and stored locally on your device and in your private Apple iCloud. You can use the "Eye" icon to hide the CVV and PIN from view, and tap the "Copy" icon to instantly copy your card number to your clipboard for online shopping.

## 🧮 The Quick Balance Calculator
Sometimes your bank charges you a small foreign transaction fee, or you earn a few pennies of interest, and your app balance drifts slightly out of sync with your real bank. 

You don't need to log a fake transaction to fix this! 
1. Go to the **Accounts** tab.
2. Tap the **Balance Number** of any account.
3. A large Quick Calculator will slide up. Simply type in the new, exact balance from your banking app, hit **Update Balance**, and the app will instantly correct itself.

## 🚐 Vehicles as Fixed Assets
At the top of your Accounts list, you might notice a **Vehicles** section. 
Because your van is likely your biggest financial asset, the app automatically pulls your van's current value directly from your [[Vehicle Specifications|Van Profile]]! Just like a bank account, you can decide whether your van's value should be included in your Net Worth and Runway calculations.
