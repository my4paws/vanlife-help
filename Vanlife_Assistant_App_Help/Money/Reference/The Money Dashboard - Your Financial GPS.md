# 🚐 The Money Dashboard: Your Financial Command Centre

Living on the road means your expenses are rarely predictable. One week you’re boondocking for free in a national forest, and the next you’re shelling out for a sudden alternator repair or an expensive ferry crossing. 

The **Money Dashboard** isn’t just a static list of numbers—it is your financial crystal ball. It takes all the scattered pieces of your vanlife finances and turns them into a beautiful, easy-to-read command centre. It tells you exactly where you are, where you’re bleeding cash, and how far your current funds will take you.

Let's break down exactly what each card on your dashboard is telling you, and why it matters for your journey.

---

## 💧 Net Worth & Your "Liquid" Nest Egg
**The Question:** *How much money do I actually have left from what I started with?*

At the very top of your dashboard sits the Hero card. This gives you your total **Net Worth**—a master number combining all your accounts and the value of your vehicle. 

But beneath that is where the magic happens: the **Nest Egg Tracker**.
* **The Liquid Wave:** If you set a "Starting Nest Egg" in your settings, this beautiful animated doughnut chart fills with a gently rolling "liquid" wave to represent the funds you have remaining. 
* **Growth vs. Burn:** It visually separates exactly how much of your original trip fund is empty space (used) versus how much is still in the tank. If you've been working on the road and made *more* than you started with, the app instantly recognizes this and shifts to show your total growth in the green!

> ✨ **Magic Feature: The Quick Flip**
> Tap anywhere on the Net Worth card! The entire card physically flips over in 3D to instantly reveal a mini-ledger of your most recent transactions. No need to dig through menus just to check if yesterday's grocery run cleared.

---

## 📈 The 12-Month Financial Heartbeat 
**The Question:** *Are my seasonal spending habits sustainable?*

Vanlife is seasonal. You might spend practically nothing during a quiet summer in the mountains, but burn through cash driving south for the winter. The **Burn Rate Chart** visualizes this entire cycle.

* **The Heartbeat:** This card features a pulsing, animated heart icon that scales to match the rhythm of your spending. 
* **Income vs. Expenses:** It renders a clear 12-month bar chart, plotting your green income against your red expenses. 
* **The Net Flow Line:** A sweeping line cuts through the bars, showing exactly where your net cash flow sits compared to your all-time average. It instantly reveals if your current month is an anomaly or part of a dangerous trend.

---

## ⏱️ 30-Day Momentum
**The Question:** *Is my recent spending buying me more time on the road, or cutting my trip short?*

> 📖 **Definition: What is "Momentum"?** > In standard budgeting, you generally just track dollars. In Vanlife Assistant, **Momentum** represents your financial speed and direction. It measures whether your recent spending habits are accelerating your journey (buying you more days on the road) or slowing it down (burning through your funds faster than you originally planned).

This is arguably the most powerful day-to-day tool in the app. The **Momentum Card** completely ignores your long-term history and looks *only* at the last 30 days. 

* **The Slot Machine:** Using a satisfying, animated rolling counter, it calculates your daily burn rate over the last month. 
* **Time is Money:** Instead of just giving you a dollar amount, it translates your spending directly into **Time**. It tells you exactly how many *Days of Travel* you have either **Gained** (by spending less than average) or **Lost** (by overspending) this month. 
* **The Gauge:** A dynamic needle swings from red (losing time) to green (gaining time), giving you an immediate gut-check on your current financial trajectory.

---

## ⚖️ Plan vs. Reality
**The Question:** *Am I actually sticking to my budget?*

It’s easy to build a theoretical budget, but reality hits hard when the alternator fails. The **Plan vs. Reality Card** constantly grades your actual bank balance against your pre-planned Scenarios.

* **The Variance:** Using a visual set of snapping, colour-coded bars, it compares your exact tracking days against your "Realistic" (or Optimistic/Pessimistic) scenarios. 
* **Runway Impact:** If you are over budget, the card doesn't just say "You are down £400." It translates that variance directly into your **Runway Impact**—showing you exactly how many days of future travel that £400 just cost you. 

> 💡 **Pro-Tip: Smart Averages**
> The dashboard's math engine is smart enough to ignore "empty days" at the very end of a month when calculating your recent averages. It dynamically spans its calculations between your actual first and last transaction dates, ensuring your daily burn rate is always a true reflection of your spending—never artificially deflated!

---

## 🔮 The 10-Year Runway Horizon
**The Question:** *Exactly when do we need to stop and find jobs?*

> 📖 **Definition: What is a "Runway Horizon"?**
> Think of an airplane's runway: it is the distance the plane has before it runs out of tarmac. In Vanlife Assistant, your **Runway** is the exact amount of time you have left before your money hits zero, assuming your current spending rate continues. The **Horizon** visualises this exact drop-dead date on a timeline, showing you when your funds will dry up.

The crown jewel of the dashboard is the **10-Year Forecast**. 

* **Liquid Runway Only:** You can't buy diesel with your van's steering wheel! The forecast intelligently strips away the physical value of your vehicle and *only* uses your liquid cash to predict your future.
* **Projected Ages & Time Until Empty:** It projects your liquid funds up to 10 years into the future, comparing your "Actual" trajectory against your Optimistic and Pessimistic scenarios. It even looks at your traveler profiles to tell you exactly how old you (and your travel partners) will be when the funds finally run dry.

---

## 🚐 Vehicle Health Status
**The Question:** *Is the van going to cost me money soon?*

Your vehicle is likely your biggest asset and your biggest liability. The dashboard integrates directly with your vehicle's maintenance schedule.

* **Data-Driven Health:** By analyzing your logged ledger entries, the app calculates your *Actual Average Daily Kilometers*. 
* **Priority Alerts:** It uses your real-world driving habits to predict when your next service is due, surfacing a Red (Overdue), Orange (Due Soon), or Green (All Systems Go) alert right on your money dashboard before a broken part destroys your budget.
