# 📥 Importing Your History - The CSV Wizard

You shouldn't have to leave your financial history behind just because you've upgraded your tools. Whether you’ve been tracking your life in a spreadsheet, using an app like TrailWallet, or you want to pull in a year of bank statements, the **CSV Import Wizard** is your bridge to a perfect 10-year forecast.

The magic of the Wizard is that it doesn't care how your old data is formatted. It teaches the app how to read *your* specific file, ensuring every grocery run and fuel stop from the last five years is accounted for.

---

## 🛠️ Step 1: Prepare Your File
Before you start, ensure your data is in a `.csv` format. Most banks and budgeting apps have an "Export to CSV" button. 

> 💡 **Pro-Tip: Keep the Headers**
> Your file is easier to import if the first row contains labels (like "Date", "Amount", "Store"). Don't worry if the names don't match our app—you'll "map" them in the next step!

---

## 📂 Step 2: Start the Wizard
1. Open the **Money** tab and navigate to **Settings** (the gear icon).
2. Tap **Import Data (CSV)**.
3. Select your file from the iPhone/iPad **Files** app. 

The Wizard will now open and show you a preview of your raw data.

---

## 🧩 Step 3: Teaching the App Your Language (Mapping)
This is where the engine gets to work. You will see a list of fields that the app needs to function (Date, Amount, Vendor, etc.). Next to each one, you will pick the column from *your* file that matches.

* **The Essentials:** You must map the **Date** and **Amount** columns at a minimum.
* **Smart Mapping:** Map the **Vendor** (Store) and **Memo** (Notes) columns to keep your history searchable.
* **Category Translation:** If your old file has categories, map them! The Wizard is smart enough to try and match your old categories to your new ones automatically.

> ✨ **Magic Feature: Multi-Currency Logic**
> If your export contains transactions in multiple currencies (like a summer trip through Europe), you can map the **Currency Code** column. The app will automatically use its internal exchange rate "Oracle" to convert those old Euros or Pesos into your home currency!

---

## 🛡️ Step 4: The Safety Filter (Review)
Before a single transaction is saved to your ledger, the app runs a **Pre-Flight Check**. 

* **The Preview:** You will see a list of every transaction the app is about to import. 
* **Duplicate Detection:** The engine looks for transactions with the same date and amount to prevent you from accidentally double-counting your expenses.
* **Final Destination:** You can choose exactly which **Account** these transactions should be imported into (e.g., your "Old Spreadsheet" account or your "Main Bank").

---

## ✅ Step 5: Execute and Forecast
Once you hit **Import**, the app processes the data locally on your device. Within seconds, your **Money Dashboard** will update.

Suddenly, those "12-Month Heartbeat" charts and "10-Year Runway" forecasts aren't just guesses—they are powered by your real-world history.
