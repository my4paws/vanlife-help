# 🌍 Currency & Sync: Travel Without Borders

Living in a van often means crossing borders, changing SIM cards, and dealing with a handful of different currencies in a single week. We built Vanlife Assistant to handle these transitions silently in the background, so you can focus on the view instead of the math.

---

## 💱 The Currency "Oracle"
When you're Wild camping in the mountains of Norway or grabbing a coffee in Morocco, you shouldn't have to manually look up exchange rates. 

* **Automated Detection:** When you start a new entry, the app uses your location to suggest the local currency automatically. 
* **The Exchange Rate Oracle:** The app has a dedicated "Oracle" that remembers the rates you use. If you manually enter an exchange rate for a purchase in Euros today, the app will remember that rate for the rest of your trip, ensuring your [[The Money Dashboard - Your Financial GPS|Daily Burn Rate]] stays consistent even when you're offline.
* **Home vs. Foreign:** Everything in the app is ultimately calculated in your **Home Currency**. This ensures your [[The Money Dashboard - Your Financial GPS|money dashboard]] is always comparing "apples to apples," no matter how many different currencies you’ve spent that month.

---

## ☁️ Seamless iCloud Sync
Your financial life shouldn't be trapped on a single device. Whether you're logging a fuel stop on your iPhone or deep-diving into your 10-year plan on your iPad, your data stays perfectly in sync.

* **SwiftData Integration:** The app uses the latest Apple technology to securely sync your ledger, accounts, and [[Milestones - Goals and Predicted Landmarks|Milestones]] across all your devices using your private iCloud account.
* **Offline First:** We know vanlife takes you off the grid. You can add transactions, edit scenarios, and manage your van's manifest while completely offline. The second you hit a pocket of 4G or find a cafe with Wi-Fi, the app will silently merge your changes with the cloud.
* **Privacy by Design:** Because we use iCloud, your data is encrypted and private to you. We never see your bank balances, your location history, or your travel plans.

---

## 🛡️ Data Integrity
We take the safety of your data seriously. 
* **Automatic Backups:** Every time your device performs a system backup, your Vanlife Assistant database is included.
* **Spreadsheet Peace of Mind:** For the ultimate backup, we always recommend using the [[Exporting Your Data (CSV)|CSV Export]] feature once a month to keep a hard copy of your history on your laptop or external drive.
