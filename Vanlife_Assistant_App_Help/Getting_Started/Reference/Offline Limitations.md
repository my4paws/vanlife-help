# 🛰️ Offline Limitations

Vanlife Assistant is built **offline-first**, which is exactly what you want when you are parked up in the woods, halfway over a mountain pass, or somewhere with one weak bar of signal that keeps disappearing.

Most of the app keeps working beautifully without internet.

If you are new to the app, **[[Welcome to Vanlife Assistant]]** is the best place to start.

---

## What works offline

Your core travel data lives on your device, so these parts of the app continue to work normally without a connection:

- Trips and check-ins
- your timeline and travel history
- Money and ledger data
- Manifest and gear tracking
- Glovebox documents
- vehicle information
- many voice interactions

For the bigger setup picture, see **[[Onboarding Guide]]** and **[[Sync and Backups]]**.

---

## What may need internet

A few features rely on live services or online sources.

### Weather

Forecasts need internet to refresh with current conditions.  
See **[[Trips/Weather/Index]]**.

### Spot and data downloads

If you are importing or downloading larger location datasets, you may need internet at the point of download.  
See **[[Spots/Index]]**.

### Syncing between devices

You can still enter data while offline, then let it sync later when a connection returns.  
That is covered in **[[Sync and Backups]]**.

---

## Why this matters

Life on the road does not happen in perfect signal conditions.

The app is intentionally designed so you can still:
- log journeys
- track spending
- manage gear
- read documents
- capture memories

...even when you are fully off-grid.

To see how that helps with travel logging, read **[[The Magic of Check-ins - Automated Journaling]]**.
