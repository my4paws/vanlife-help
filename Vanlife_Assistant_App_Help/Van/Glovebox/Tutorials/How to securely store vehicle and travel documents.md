# How to securely store vehicle and travel documents 📄

Living in a van means space is at a premium, and losing a physical paper document like an insurance certificate or a passport can be a nightmare. The Digital Glovebox allows you to digitise, organise, and track every important piece of paper in your life.

## 🗂️ How Organisation Works
Instead of a messy, single list of files, the Glovebox organises your documents by **Owner Scope**:
* **Vehicles:** Documents tied to your van (e.g., MOT, V5C, Vehicle Insurance).
* **Travellers:** Personal documents for specific people (e.g., Passports, Visas, Medical cards).
* **Finance / Receipts:** Proof of purchase and warranties.
* **Inventory / Junk Drawer:** Manuals for your appliances, or random bits of paper you aren't sure where to put.
* **Custom Containers:** You can even build your own custom folders for specific trips or needs!

---

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step: Adding a Document

1. **Open the Glovebox:** Navigate to the Glovebox tab in the app.
2. **Tap the '+' Button:** Tap the floating action button in the bottom corner.
3. **Choose the Owner:** The app will ask *where* this document belongs. Select your Vehicle, a specific Traveller, or a custom container.
4. **Attach the File:** Choose how to attach the document:
   * **Scan:** Use the camera to create a multi-page PDF (Great for contracts).
   * **Photo:** Pick an existing image from your camera roll.
   * **File:** Import a PDF directly from iCloud or your phone's files.
5. **Fill in the Details:**
   * **Title:** Give it a clear name (e.g., "Van Insurance 2026").
   * **Category:** Pick a color-coded category (like Legal, ID, or Service).
   * **Expiry Date:** *Crucial!* If this document expires, toggle this on and set the date. The app will automatically track this and warn you when it is due!
   * **Tags:** Add tags like "Border" or "Medical" to make finding it later instant.
6. **Tap Save.**

## ⏰ Expiring Soon & Alerts
If you set an Expiry Date on a document, you never have to worry about forgetting to renew it. 
* The app will automatically place it in an **Expiring Soon** section at the top of your Glovebox when the date approaches (showing an orange or red warning badge).
* The app will send you push notifications before your MOT, Insurance, or Passport runs out.

## 🏷️ Pro-Tip: Using Tags
Tags are incredibly powerful. You can tag a document with a **Context** (e.g., "Border", "Emergency"), a **Type** (e.g., "Passport / ID"), or a **Workflow** (e.g., "Needs Renewal"). 
Tap the "Filter" icon at the top of the Glovebox to instantly pull up every document across your entire van that shares a specific tag!
