# ⚙️ Settings Help Centre

The Settings hub is the control centre for Vanlife Assistant. Because the app acts as your co-pilot, it needs to know how you travel, who you travel with, and how you prefer to measure things. 

From the main Settings tab, you can manage your Crew (adding humans and pets to your manifest), configure your Fleet of vehicles, and fine-tune the core engines (Money, Trips, Voice, and Smart Van).
